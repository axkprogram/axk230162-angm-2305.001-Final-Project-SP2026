The beginning:

The player is an adventurer accompanied by a mage and a rogue.

The three were hired by a noble to find an old ancient temple, and to find an artifact within the temple to bring back to the noble.

The player character has decided to search for the temple inside of a cave they stumbled upon by accident.

And by that I mean they were startled by a creature in the area and fell into a hidden cave

They are accompanied by two other characters
(first dialogue options will come from interacting with them, and will affect their favor of the player, but not where the story will go)

The three of them will traverse the cavern and find a fork in the road, there are three paths, each will lead to different ends, and different parts of the same temple. 

The fighter will choose the path with a gravel path (called the fighter path)

The mage will choose the stone paved path(called the mage path)

The third path is a dark one(called the player path)


The Fighter Path:

If the player chooses the fighter path:
Fighter gains favor
Mage loses favor

Dialogue is necessary to convince the mage to go down the fighter path
This requires a dice roll to determine what favor is lost or gained
1 = -2 favor
2-8 = -1 favor
9-12 = 0 favor
13-19 = +1 favor
20 = +2 favor
But the mage will follow the other two regardless

There will be dialogue(mentions the players bell) as the three move down the path when they’re blocked by a hound

The hound is an enemy that is only affected by attacks with a bladed weapon. The fighter jumps into a battle with the hound.

Once the hound is defeated, the Fighter and Player obtain an item. 

The mage can analyze the item
Mage gains favor
Fighter is neutral
The item from the fighter path is revealed to be a talisman of sight
		
		The Fighter Temple Door:
The talisman can be placed on the pedestal to open the door
The door will open and the chapter will end
(The fighter gains favor)

Or

The group can turn back and take the talisman as the artifact for the noble
They will leave and the chapter will end
(the mage gains favor)


Fighter can destroy the item
Fighter gains favor
Mage loses favor
The fighter breaks the item and inside is a red gemstone
		
		Fighter Temple Door:
			Gemstone can be placed on the door to open the door
Chapter will end
(The fighter gains favor)

Or

(The fighter loses favor)
