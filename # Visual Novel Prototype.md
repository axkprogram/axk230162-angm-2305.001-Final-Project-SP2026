## Description
I got into gaming with art and story so it would kind of be a culmination of creating an interesting story with pretty art with a game loop possible with python code. A visual novel is essentially a choose your own story game, it's not entirely text based with some special effects and collectibles and minigames, as well as image animation, so hopefully this works as a game and not a text based adventure.

## Features
- Story choices and complex story trails
	- There will be one story, but there will be multiple trails using if then, else elif, libraries and lists
- Rolling for luck
	- Dungeons and Dragons mechnaic of a dice determining your level of success when choosing specific choices, like when a player is trying to decieve or persuade another character, groups of numbers will result in a different deviation of story
- Rpg battle within the visual novel
	- turn based battling enemies if there is the scenario calls for it or it's a random encounter.
- character creator

## Challenges
- creating the basic story trail
- coding the rpg battle
- animating the characters in the novel/ character creation
- creating buttons with text inlaid in them
- game type transition from the visual novel to the rpg battle, back to the visual novel
